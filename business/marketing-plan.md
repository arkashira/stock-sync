# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary:** "AI-powered inventory planning for high-SKU DTC brands to optimize stock levels and minimize waste."
**Alternatives:**
1. "Smart stock optimization for DTC brands with extensive SKU catalogs."
2. "AI-driven inventory management for direct-to-consumer brands to reduce overstock and waste."
3. "Automated inventory planning for high-SKU DTC businesses to maximize profitability."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. **Personas:**
   - **Alex, Inventory Manager:** Daily job involves managing stock levels, forecasting demand, and coordinating with suppliers. Budget: $5,000/month.
   - **Sarah, Operations Director:** Oversees supply chain operations, ensures inventory efficiency, and manages budgets. Budget: $10,000/month.
   - **Mike, E-commerce Manager:** Focuses on product availability, customer satisfaction, and reducing waste. Budget: $7,500/month.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads:** Target inventory managers, operations directors, and e-commerce managers. CAC estimate: $150.
2. **Google Ads:** Focus on keywords related to inventory management and DTC brands. CAC estimate: $120.
3. **Industry Webinars:** Partner with e-commerce and supply chain platforms. CAC estimate: $200.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Blog posts on inventory management challenges, case studies, and product demos.
- **Week 5-8:** Webinars on AI-driven inventory optimization, customer testimonials, and ROI analysis.
- **Week 9-12:** Email campaigns with success stories, product updates, and exclusive offers.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing materials, set up ad campaigns, and prepare webinar content.
- **D-0:** Official product launch, kick off ad campaigns, and host the first webinar.
- **D+30:** Analyze initial user feedback, adjust marketing strategies, and launch email campaigns.
- **D+90:** Evaluate success metrics, scale successful channels, and plan for the next phase.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Target 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** Target $50,000 MRR by D+90.
