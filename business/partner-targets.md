## Partner Integration Roadmap

This document outlines the integration roadmap for the `stock-sync` AI-powered inventory planning tool, focusing on strategic SaaS/API partnerships that will enhance the product's value proposition and user experience.

### 1. Shopify ([Integration Effort: L] - [Value-Add: H])
Shopify is a leading e-commerce platform for small and medium-sized businesses, offering a comprehensive suite of tools for online store management. Integrating `stock-sync` with Shopify will enable seamless inventory synchronization, reducing manual effort and minimizing stock discrepancies.

- Free-tier limits: Up to 2 staff accounts, 4 sales channels, and 25 products.
- Revenue-share: Shopify offers a revenue-share program for select partners.

### 2. Stripe ([Integration Effort: M] - [Value-Add: H])
Stripe is a popular payment processing platform that supports businesses of all sizes. Integrating `stock-sync` with Stripe will allow for seamless payment processing and financial management, providing valuable insights for inventory planning and optimization.

- Free-tier limits: Up to $1M in annual processing volume.
- Revenue-share: Stripe offers a revenue-share program for select partners.

### 3. Algolia ([Integration Effort: M] - [Value-Add: M])
Algolia is a search API that provides fast and relevant search experiences for e-commerce businesses. Integrating `stock-sync` with Algolia will improve the search functionality of the inventory planning tool, making it easier for users to find and manage their products.

- Free-tier limits: Up to 1M API requests per month.
- Revenue-share: Algolia offers a revenue-share program for select partners.

### 4. Zapier ([Integration Effort: S] - [Value-Add: M])
Zapier is a powerful tool that allows for seamless integration between various SaaS applications. Integrating `stock-sync` with Zapier will enable users to connect their inventory planning tool with other services they use, such as CRM, marketing, and analytics platforms.

- Free-tier limits: Up to 5 Zaps, 100 tasks per month.
- Revenue-share: Zapier offers a revenue-share program for select partners.

### 5. Google Analytics ([Integration Effort: S] - [Value-Add: H])
Google Analytics is a comprehensive web analytics service that provides insights into user behavior and website performance. Integrating `stock-sync` with Google Analytics will allow users to track the impact of their inventory planning decisions on key performance indicators, such as conversion rates and revenue.

- Free-tier limits: Unlimited data retention, up to 10M hits per month.
- Revenue-share: Google Analytics does not offer a revenue-share program.

### 6. Mailchimp ([Integration Effort: S] - [Value-Add: M])
Mailchimp is an all-in-one marketing platform that helps businesses manage their email marketing, social media, and landing pages. Integrating `stock-sync` with Mailchimp will enable users to automate marketing campaigns based on inventory levels and sales data, improving overall marketing efficiency.

- Free-tier limits: Up to 2,000 contacts, 10,000 emails per month.
- Revenue-share: Mailchimp offers a revenue-share program for select partners.

### 7. QuickBooks ([Integration Effort: M] - [Value-Add: H])
QuickBooks is a popular accounting software that helps businesses manage their finances. Integrating `stock-sync` with QuickBooks will allow for seamless financial management, including invoicing, expense tracking, and tax compliance.

- Free-tier limits: Up to 5 users, 500 invoices per year, 100 bills per month.
- Revenue-share: QuickBooks does not offer a revenue-share program.

By integrating with these strategic partners, `stock-sync` will provide a more comprehensive solution for businesses with high-SKU, DTC assortments, addressing their inventory planning and management needs while minimizing waste and optimizing stock levels.
